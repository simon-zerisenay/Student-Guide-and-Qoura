ZU Help App
This app is created to provide help to Zayed university male students. It provides the students with the full location and information about the important destinations inside the university in different languages which they are comfortable with. Moreover, there is an online service in which a student can post a question and get instant answers from other students. Besides, students can use this app to report any issues or problems to the staffs in charge from a given list.
